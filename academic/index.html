About

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science and Cyber Security at Augusta University, researching the intersection of mixed reality, computer vision, robotics, and trustworthy AI. My current focus is on scalable perception pipelines for immersive devices and resilience against acoustic side-channel attacks.

Previously, I engineered production-grade vision systems for industrial inspection and led robotics initiatives that bridged prototypes to field deployments. I collaborate closely with interdisciplinary teams to deliver secure, human-centered intelligent systems.
