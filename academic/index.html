Ph.D. Student · Computer Science & Cyber Security · Augusta University

I design trustworthy mixed reality systems that blend perceptual computing, artificial intelligence, and human-centered security. My research spans scalable 3D object detection pipelines, incremental learning for AR devices, and safeguards against acoustic side-channel attacks.

Before joining Augusta University, I engineered production-grade computer vision frameworks for industrial inspection and built robotic platforms that bridged prototyping and deployment. Today I collaborate closely with interdisciplinary teams to deliver immersive, explainable, and secure AI experiences.
