About Me

I am currently a Ph.D. student in Computer Science and Cyber Security at Augusta University - Georgia Cyber Center. My research focuses on the integration of artificial intelligence (AI) and computer vision with Extended Reality (XR) under the supervision of Dr. Jason Orlosky at the AR-VR lab. I am also exploring cybersecurity concepts, particularly side-channel attacks.

Prior to my doctoral studies, I worked as a Machine Learning Engineer at Experts Group (AIEX) in Montreal, Canada, where I developed AI-based frameworks for computer vision applications on edge devices and GPU servers. I hold a Master's degree in Electrical and Control Engineering from Bu-Ali Sina University, where I focused on vehicle detection and classification using computer vision techniques.

I am passionate about advancing research in mixed reality, computer vision, robotics, and AI cyber security. I am actively looking for collaboration opportunities and am interested in exploring innovative solutions that bridge the gap between virtual and physical environments.
