About

Alireza Taheritajar is a Ph.D. student in Computer Science and Cyber Security at Augusta University, where he works with Dr. Jason Orlosky in the AR-VR laboratory. His research focuses on the intersection of AI-driven perception and secure extended reality systems, with applications to immersive training, language tutoring, and resilient situational awareness.

Prior to Augusta University, Alireza led machine learning engineering efforts in both industry and academia, building scalable computer vision solutions for edge devices and intelligent robotics platforms.
